About System

This is a service for emergency management system based on AI for elderly and paralysis patients who use our hardware based IOT product Health-X.This is based on the algorithm to detect the local ambulance availability for patients in a particular location we use GPS Technology to serve the purpose of the problem of facilitating the working of ambulance as of emergency management system.The Users can track and manage the outgoing service of ambulance within their user id and password get the actual location of hospital ambulance alloted.We use AI technology to track the details and use data in a structured manner to solve our issue.The service is built with django backend and sqlite as database system
